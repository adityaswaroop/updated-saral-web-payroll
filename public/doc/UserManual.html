User Manual – Web Payroll


Following are the steps to be followed to set up the Web Payroll application initially.


  1. Log in by providing username as “admin” and password as “default” or any other appropriate credentials.




  1. Make the following settings as shown:


Create a new pay month from when you wish to start the payroll process

Settings>Pay Months






Create a list of states which will be used in employees addresses and PT groups


Settings>States




Create all the Designations in the company

Settings>Designations




Create all the Departments in the company

Settings>Departments





Create all the Grades in the company

Settings>Grades




  1. Since there will be no company created, after logging in, click on Company, you should be able see a link “Create a new company”. Click on that link and enter the details of the company and save as shown:


Click on the link “Create a new Company”




Enter the required details in the fields provided




PT Rates: Create PT rates by providing the required details

Company>PT Rates – Click on “Add New PT Rate”




PF ESI Rates: Create PF ESI rates by providing the required details

Company>PF ESI Rates – Click on “Add New PF ESI Rate”




  1. Salary Settings

Salary group master: Create at least one Salary Group by providing the required details as shown:

Salary>Salary Group – Click on “Add New Salary Group”




Salary head master: Create at least one earning head and at least one deduction head as shown:

Salary>Salary heads – Click on “New Salary Head”





Configure each salary group by assigning heads to each of them as shown:

Salary>Salary Configuration – Click on “Add New Salary Group Conf.”



Add an employee as shown:

Master>Employees – Click on “Add New Employee”





Employees can also be uploaded from an Excel file as shown:

Master>Employees – Click on “Excel Upload”




The details of employee's salary groups can be added by clicking on the “Details” button of each employee record in the employees list.


Following are the steps to be followed for processing the salary.


Salary Allotment: Salaries should be allotted to all the employees for whom salaries should be processed.


Salary>Salary Allotment



Enter the salaries of each employee and click on Allot Salary button.


Then go to Salary>Salary Editor


Select the employee and month, click on get salary.



Change the amounts if required and click on save salary.


This will redirect to Salary generation page





Select the employee's name and month, click on Generate Salary to get the processed salary.




Click on the link “Generate Payslip” to get the payslip in PDF format.
